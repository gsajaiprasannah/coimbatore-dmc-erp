Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

How far in advance should I book? +
For peak season (October – February), we recommend booking at least 3–4 weeks in advance, especially for wildlife permits at Topslip which have limited availability. Off-season bookings can be done with as little as 1 week's notice.
Do you handle airport/railway transfers? +
Yes — all our packages include airport and railway pickups and drops. We have a fleet of AC cabs and tempo travellers for groups of all sizes. Just share your flight/train details and we'll handle the rest.
Can I get a package for just 1 or 2 people? +
Absolutely! We specialise in small-group and couple tours. Some of our most popular packages are specifically designed for couples on honeymoon or solo travellers. Pricing for solo or duo bookings is adjusted accordingly.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), and cash. A 25–50% advance confirms the booking, with the balance due 48 hours before departure. We'll send you an official invoice for every transaction.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Cancellations 14+ days before departure: full refund minus processing fees. 7–13 days: 50% refund. Under 7 days: no refund, but we can reschedule within 6 months. Please contact us as early as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
Do you organise corporate / MICE tours? +
Yes, corporate team outings are one of our specialties. We've handled groups from 10 to 300 people. We manage everything from transport and accommodation to team-building activities, conference setups, and gala dinners. Contact us for a dedicated corporate quote.
